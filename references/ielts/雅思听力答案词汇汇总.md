## 官方样题确认的题型

IELTS.org 官方样题 PDF 覆盖这些听力题型：

- Form **completion**：表格/表单填空
- Note / table / flow-chart / summary completion：笔记、表格、流程图、摘要填空
- Sentence completion：句子填空
- Short-answer questions：简答题
- Multiple choice：选择题
- Matching：匹配题
- Plan / map / diagram labelling：地图、平面图、图表标注

机考重点：答案输入必须注意拼写、单复数、数字格式、货币符号和大小写。大小写通常不扣分，但拼写和词形错误会扣分。

## Part 1：日常信息类答案词

场景：电话咨询、报名、预订、租房、课程登记、个人信息表。

### 日期与时间

- Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
- January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December
- spring, summer, autumn, winter
- morning, afternoon, evening, weekday, weekend
- appointment, deadline, schedule, timetable

高频错误：

- Wednesday 中间的 `d` 容易漏。
- February 第一个 `r` 容易漏。
- autumn 末尾 `n` 不发音但要写。
- 13 和 30 要听重音：thirTEEN vs THIRty。

### 数字、金额、地址

- postcode, address, apartment, flat, floor, block, building
- street, road, avenue, lane, drive, court, terrace
- dollars, pounds, euros, fee, deposit, refund, discount
- single room, double room, shared room

机考策略：

- 能写数字就写数字：`30`, `10,000`, `6:00`, `$30`。
- 货币符号要和题目一致：`$`、`£`、`€` 不要混。
- 邮编、电话、门牌号要逐字符核对。

### 交通与出行

- train, tram, subway, bus, coach, taxi, bicycle, pedestrian
- passenger, commuter, platform, station, terminal
- ticket, route, transfer, departure, arrival

易混：

- commuter 不是 computer。
- passenger 有双 `s`。

### 常见形容词和动词

- necessary, convenient, comfortable, permanent, temporary
- difficult, wonderful, terrible, knowledgeable
- suggest, borrow, persuade, discuss, review, concentrate, believe

注意：

- comfortable 不要写成 `comftable`。
- necessary 常错在 `c` / `s` 数量。
- believe 遵循 `ie` 拼写。

## Part 2：地图、设施、场所类答案词

场景：导览、社区设施介绍、校园建筑、博物馆、体育中心、地图题。

### 建筑与房间

- reception, entrance, exit, ticket desk
- kitchen, bathroom, bedroom, dining room, lounge
- library, gym, cafeteria, classroom, theatre
- storage room, waiting room, laboratory

### 地图方位

- north, south, east, west
- left, right, straight, corner, opposite
- across from, between, beside, adjacent to
- near, past, before, after, diagonal

### 地图动作词

- turn, move, continue, cross, pass
- start, finish, stop, go straight ahead

### 常见地点

- tennis court, basketball court, swimming pool
- river, bridge, path, traffic lights
- bench, garden, courtyard, forest, beach

机考策略：

- 做地图题前在草稿纸/心里标出 left/right，避免紧张时左右混。
- 如果题目没有方向标，自己先定位入口、北方或主要路径。
- 地图题很多答案不是“难词”，而是能否跟上顺序。

## Part 3：校园讨论与学术任务

场景：学生讨论作业、导师反馈、项目分工、课程安排。

### 学习与作业

- presentation, project, assignment, outline
- teamwork, questionnaire, experiment, reference
- proofreading, attendance, tutorial, lecture
- tutor, instructor, supervisor, specialist

### 学校与学科

- mathematics, science, English, geography, biology
- chemistry, history, art, music, physical education
- psychology, engineering, sociology, medicine
- architecture, law, philosophy, economics, education

### 考试与评价

- assessment, revision, test, exam
- pass, fail, retake, evaluate, results
- degree, certificate, bachelor’s, master’s
- percentage, statistic, faculty, semester

注意：

- laboratory 美式/英式发音不同，但拼写不变。
- questionnaire 很容易拼错。
- bachelor’s / master’s 有所有格形式，但听力答案通常按题目要求写。

## Part 4：学术讲座主题词

场景：单人学术讲座，通常信息密度高，答案多为名词、形容词、专业主题词。

### 健康与医学

- vegetarian, vegan, obesity, overweight
- treatment, medication, check-up, vaccination
- vitamin, protein, carbohydrates
- pandemic, virus, remedy, well-being

### 动物与生态

- mammals, reptiles, primates
- predators, prey, endangered species
- habitat, island, jungle, forest, ocean

### 环境与灾害

- global warming, pollution, temperature
- drought, flood, cyclone, hurricane
- earthquake, volcanic eruption
- deforestation, desertification, bushfire

### 政府与社会

- government, politics, politician, mayor
- regulation, law, council, president
- society, individual, community

### 能源

- nuclear, oil, coal, natural gas
- solar power, hydroelectric power
- wind turbine, dam, renewable, non-renewable
- electricity generation, energy source

高频错误：

- nuclear 不要写成 unclear。
- government 中间有 `n`。
- society 是 `socie-ty`，不要漏字母。
- different 双 `f`，不是 `diferent`。

## 机考练习方法

每天 20 分钟：

1. 听写 20 个词：日期、地点、学术词混合。
2. 手动输入答案，不要只在脑子里过。
3. 核对拼写、单复数、数字格式。
4. 把错词加入 `错词本`。

每周 2 次：

1. 做一套官方或 Cambridge 听力。
2. 只复盘填空题和地图题的错因。
3. 把错因归类为：没听到、听到了但拼错、单复数错、同义替换没识别、定位丢失。
