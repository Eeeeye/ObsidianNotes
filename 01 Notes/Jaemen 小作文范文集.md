## 一、线性图入门：CO2排放4国（ep1）

**题目**：
> The graph below shows average carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions per person in the United Kingdom, Sweden, Italy and Portugal between 1967 and 2007.

**真实数据**：
- UK：11吨→10吨→9吨，缓慢下降，全程最高
- Sweden：9吨→10吨(1977峰值)→7吨→5吨，先升后降
- Italy：4吨→6吨→7.5吨→7.5吨，先升后稳
- Portugal：1.5吨→5吨→5吨，持续上升至持平

**Introduction**：
```
Given is a graph illustrating the production of four kinds of vehicles in the United Kingdom from 1995 to 2005.
→ A graph illustrating how the production...changed over a 10-year period...is given.
```

**Overview**：
> Overall, it is clear that the amount of CO2 in Italy and Portugal showed a dramatic increase, while the figures for Sweden and the UK showed a downward trend. The UK produced the largest amount of CO2 throughout the period.

**完整范文（4段）**：

Paragraph 1（UK—最高值）：
> Commencing with the UK, the figure for it almost stayed unchanged over the first decade at nearly 11 tons, after which it steadily declined to about 9 tons in 2007. Despite this decline, the average carbon dioxide emissions in the UK were still the largest among the four countries throughout the period.

Paragraph 2（Sweden—段内大小等过渡）：
> The figure for Sweden increased from 9 tons in 1967 to 10 tons, and then it declined significantly to 7 tons in 1987. Over the next two decades, it slightly decreased to nearly 5 tons. A similar trend can also be found in Sweden. The figure for it increased from 9 tons to 10 tons, after which it steadily declined to about 5 tons in 2007.

Paragraph 3（Italy+Portugal—段间过渡）：
> By contrast, the figure for Italy increased from just more than 4 tons in 1967 to more than 6 tons in 1977, and then it grew slightly to nearly 7.5 tons in 1997, outnumbering that for Sweden in 1990. Over the next decade, it had levelled off. Similarly, the figure for Portugal increased from approximately 1.5 tons to 5 tons over the first three decades, after which it remained unchanged, equaling that for Sweden in 2007.

## 二、线性图上天：墨尔本社交中心（ep2）

**题目**：
> The graph below gives information on the numbers of participants for different activities at one social centre in Melbourne, Australia for the period 2000 to 2020.

**真实数据**：
- Film club：60-65（最高）
- Martial arts：35-40 稳定
- Amateur dramatics：25→5（下降）
- Table tennis：15→50（上升）
- Musical performances：0→15（2005后出现）

**Introduction（Given倒装）**：
> Given is a graph illustrating how the number of participants for different activities changed at one social center in Melbourne, Australia over a 20-year period from 2000 to 2020.

**Overview 批改对比**：

| 版本 | 范文 |
|------|------|
| 普通 | The number of participants in table tennis and musical performances showed an upward trend, while the figure for Amateur dramatics showed a decline. The number of film club was the largest throughout the period |
| 7.5+ | The popularity of table tennis and musical performances had grown significantly while amateur dramatics became less attractive. Film club was the most popular activity throughout the period |
| ⭐5星 | The popularity of table tennis and musical performances **has** grown significantly while amateur dramatics became less attractive, **with film club being** the most popular activity throughout the period |

**完整范文（4段）**：

Paragraph 1（6.5 → 7.5+ 对比）：
> 普通：Commencing with the film club, the number of participants in this activity was the largest, and it fluctuated between 60 and 65. A similar stable trend can be found in martial arts. The number was nearly 35 in 2000, and the it fluctuated around 35.
>
> ⭐7.5+：Commencing with the film club, **participants in it accounted for the lion's share**, with its number fluctuating between 60 and 65. A similar stable trend can be found in martial arts: approximately 35 people **engaged in this activity** in 2000, and the number fluctuated around 35 people.

Paragraph 2（Although升级 + 省略性定语从句）：
> 普通：With regard to table tennis, the number of people who were interested in it remained almost unchanged at 15 over the first decade and then it soared to 50 in 2020. Similarly, the number of participants for Musical performances also grew from 0 in 2005 to nearly 15 in 2020
>
> 7.5+：With regard to table tennis, **although** the number of people interested in it remained almost unchanged at 15 over the first decade, it soared to 50 in 2020. Similarly, the popularity of musical performances also grew: **Although no one took part in it until 2005**, the number of participants had steadily increased to nearly 15 by 2020 since 2005.

省略性定语从句：`people who played → people playing`，`people who took part in → people taking part in`

Paragraph 3（with独立主格收尾）：
> By contrast, fewer people chose amateur dramatics during this period. Although the number of participants slightly increased from 25 to 28 over the first five years, it had steadily declined to only 5 by the end of the period, **with this activity being the least popular in 2020**.

## 三、饼状图入门：USA三餐营养素（ep3）

**题目**：
> The charts below show the average percentages in typical meals of three types of nutrients, all of which may be unhealthy if eaten too much.

**真实数据**：
- Sodium：dinner 43%最大，lunch 29%，snacks 14%，breakfast 14%
- Saturated fat：dinner 37%，lunch 26%，snacks 21%，breakfast 16%
- Added sugar：snacks 42%最大，dinner 19%，lunch 23%，breakfast 16%

**Introduction（What percentage变形记）**：
> Given are charts illustrating what percentage of three nutrients people consume from four different meals

变形公式：百分比+名词 → what percentage + 名词 + 动词 + from/in

**Overview（3信息点 + while/with连接）**：
> Overall, it is clear that the consumption of sodium and saturated fat is mainly from dinner while snacks tend to have more added sugar, with breakfast containing the lowest percentages of three nutrients and being the healthiest meal

**完整范文（4段）**：

Paragraph 1（大+等）：
> Commencing with sodium, Americans consume 43% of it from dinner, **as opposed to** 29% provided by lunch. The percentages of sodium from snacks and breakfast **are the same, with each accounting for** 14%.

「等」的批改：
- 普通：14% of sodium is consumed from snacks, equaling that from breakfast
- 升级：The percentages of sodium from snacks and breakfast are the same, with each accounting for 14%

Paragraph 2（小）：
> With regard to saturated fat, Dinner contributes to 37% of saturated fat, **slightly higher than** lunch, at 26%. People eat 21% of it from their snacks and the next is breakfast, at 16%.

Paragraph 3（倍）：
> Concerning added sugar, People eat 42% of added sugar from their snacks, **which is almost twice as high as that from dinner, at 23%**. Next are lunch and breakfast, at 19% and 16% respectively.

## 四、柱状图上天：澳洲咖啡茶（ep4，剑桥15 Test 2）

**题目**：
> The chart below shows the results of a survey about people's coffee and tea buying and drinking habits in five Australian cities.

**真实数据**：

| 城市 | Fresh | Instant | Café |
|------|-------|---------|------|
| Sydney | 44% | 45% | 62% |
| Melbourne | 42% | 48% | 64% |
| Brisbane | 34% | 53% | 55% |
| Adelaide | 34% | 50% | 49% (例外) |
| Hobart | 37% | 54% | 63% |

**Introduction**：
> Given is a chart illustrating how Australians' coffee and tea buying and drinking habits differ in five areas.

**Overview 批改**：

| 版本 | 范文 |
|------|------|
| 普通 | The percentage of people who went to a café was the highest, except in Adelaide. Fresh coffee was the lowest in all cities |
| 7.5+ | Overall, it is clear that **going to a café** to have coffee or tea was the **most common habit**, except in Adelaide, **with people generally preferring instant coffee to fresh coffee** |

**完整范文（3个主体段，按图例分组）**：

Paragraph 1（café visits—2-2-1分组）：
> Commencing with the choice of going to café for coffee or tea, more than 60% of people in Sydney, Melbourne, and Hobart did so in last four weeks. 55% of residents visited cafes in Brisbane and next is Adelaide, at nearly 50%.

Paragraph 2（instant coffee）：
> With regard to instant coffee, approximately 54% of people in Hobart bought instant coffee, slightly higher than that figure in Brisbane, at 53%. Next are Adelaide and Melbourne, at 50% and 48% respectively. **Ranked last was Sydney, at only nearly 45%**

Paragraph 3（fresh coffee + ⭐Compared with过渡）：
> ⭐较版：**Compared with instant coffee, fresh coffee was less popular.** Approximately 44% of people in Sydney had the habit of buying it, slightly higher than that figure in Melbourne, at 42%. Next is Hobart, at 37% and the percentages in Brisbane and Adelaide are the same, with each accounting for nearly 34%

## 五、地图题入门：大学体育中心（ep6）

**题目**：
> The plans below show the layout of a university's sports centre now, and how it will look after the redevelopment.

**地图数据**：
- 现在：中央 Gym + Reception + 25m Pool，东西 Outdoor courts + Changing room + Seating
- 未来：左侧加 Leisure pool，右侧加 Sports hall + Café + Sports shop，扩展 Gym + 2 Dance studios

**Introduction**：
> Given are two maps illustrating the current layout of a sports center and changes that will be introduced

**Overview（目的+细节）**：
> It is clear that the renovation will enable the sports center to better cater for students and meet their demand for indoor activities, with the introduction of a shop, a sports hall, and a leisure pool

**完整范文（4段）**：

Paragraph 1（现在布局—倒装句）：
> Commencing with the current layout of the sports center, **in the middle is a rectangular building, to the east and west of which are outdoor courts for outdoor activities. Inside the building, at the entrance is the reception area**

原句 → 倒装升级：`A building is in the middle and two outdoor courts are to the east and west of it.` → `In the middle is a building and to the east and west of it are two outdoor courts.`

Paragraph 2（未来变化—左→中→右空间顺序）：
> As per the redevelopment plan, the two outdoor courts will be demolished to make way for the enlargement of the sport center.
>
> A new leisure pool will be introduced on the left-hand side of the centre, next to the original changing room, while on the east of the existing seating areas, a sports hall will be built, with two new dance studios.
>
> The areas in the middle will remain unchanged, except that the gym will be expanded. In the southwest and southeast corners of the center, additional changing rooms will be added to cater for more swimmers and exercisers.
>
> Similarly, a sports shop and a café will be introduced to serve them.

## 六、地图题上天：Norbiton 工业区（ep7）

**题目**：
> The two maps below show an industrial area in Norbiton now and the planned future development of the same area.

**地图数据**：
- 现在：北 river + farmland，中 central roundabout + 4-8 factories，西 town，南 main road
- 未来：factory → housing，保留原 roundabout + 新增 mini-roundabout，新增 shops/medical center/playground/school/bridge

**Introduction**：
> Given are two maps illustrating the current layout of a town of Norbiton and what it will look like after development.

**Overview（⭐2星标 + 红笔下划线）**：
> ⭐Overall, as per the future development, this industrial estate will have been transformed into a housing estate, with construction of housing and community facilities. Accordingly, the road conditions will also improve (e.g., side roads and roundabouts)

**完整范文（4段）**：

Paragraph 1（现在布局—先上后下+先中间再道路）：
> At present, in the north of the town, a river runs from west to east, to the north of which is the farmland. In the middle of the industrial area is a roundabout, which connects to a side road and a main road in the south part of the map. Along the roundabout and the side road are eight factories.

Paragraph 2（未来变化—设施+道路二分）：
> According to the planned future layout, all the factories will be demolished to make way for housing. To meet the demand of this residential district, local amenities will be introduced. Shops and a medical center will be built along the roundbout while a playground and a school are expected to build along the original side road.
>
> **With such development will also come road improvement:** A new mini-roundabout will be introduced to connect the main road and the access road to the town. The existing roundabout, meanwhile, will connect two newly built roads - one residential road and one north-south road. The latter will connect to a new bridge across the river, **which brings convenience to local agricultural activities.**

## 七、流程图基础：方便面制造（ep8，剑桥15）

**题目**：
> The diagram below shows how instant noodles are manufactured.

**流程图 8 步**：
1. Storage silos → 2. Mixer → 3. Dough sheets → 4. Dough strips → 5. Noodles discs → 6. Cooking(oil) + drying → 7. Vegetables + spices → 8. Labelling + sealing

**Introduction（三种写法，推荐③）**：
```
① Given is a diagram illustrating how instant noodles are manufactured.
② Given is a diagram illustrating the steps involved in the production of instant noodles.
③ Given is a diagram illustrating the process by which the instant noodles are produced.（推荐）
```

**Overview**：
> Overall, it is clear that there are eight steps from flour to final instant noodles. The first stage is to make noodles and then the second stage is to package them.

**完整范文（4段）**：

Paragraph 1（制作流程，6步）：
> Commencing with the **noodle-making stage**, the first stage is that the flour is sent by cars to storage silos. The flour is sent to a mixer, **where** it is mixed with water and oil. The mixture is made into dough sheets with rollers. The sheets are made in dough strips, after which they are made into noodle discs. Next, the noodle discs are cooked with oil and then dried.

Paragraph 2（包装流程，2步）：
> With regard to the **noodle-packaging stage**, the first stage is that the noodle discs is put into cups and then the vegetable and spices are added to cups. The cup is sent to a machine, **where** it is labelled and sealed, after which the instant noodle is ready to be sold.

升级：普通 `Commencing with the first stage` → `Commencing with the noodle-making stage`（用 hyphen 构词法）

## 八、流程图上天：甘蔗制糖（ep9，剑桥16）

**题目**：
> The diagram below shows how sugar is produced from sugar cane.

**流程图 7 步**（固体→液体→糖）：
1. Growing（种植，12-18 months）→ 2. Harvesting（收割）→ 3. Crushing（压榨）→ 4. Purifying（石灰过滤）→ 5. Evaporator（加热→糖浆）→ 6. Centrifuge（离心分离晶体）→ 7. Drying and cooling

**Introduction**：
> Given is a diagram illustrating the process by which the sugar is manufactured from sugar cane.

**Overview（initial/later替代first/second）**：
> Overall, there are seven steps from sugar cane to sugar. The initial stage is to collect sugar cane to produce juice, and the later stage is to make sugar.

**完整范文（2主体段 + 无痕衔接，9分CC标准）**：

Paragraph 1（juice producing stage—3:4分割）：
> Commencing with the juice-producing stage, the first step is to plant sugar canes to allow them to grow. **Having grown** for 12-18 months, they are harvested either by industrial farming machines or by hand. After that, the **harvested** sugar canes are then sent to a crushing machine, **where** they are crushed to produce raw sugar cane juice.

Paragraph 2（sugar manufacturing stage）：
> **Next comes the manufacturing stage.** The juice is purified through a limestone filter, **which** makes it ready for the heating process. **Having been heated** in an evaporator for a period, the juice becomes syrup, **from which** the sugar crystals are separated in a centrifuge. The final step is to dry and cool the separated sugar crystals, after which sugar is made.

## 九、额外字典条目

**cater for sb/sth**（ep6）：
满足、迎合（某群人的需要）
- Vegetarians are well catered for.
- an LA bank catering to Asian businesses

**demand** /dɪˈmɑːnd/（ep6）：
noun — 需要，需求
- Production is still increasing faster than demand.
- the demand for new housing

**as per sth** — formal（ep6）：
按照某事物
- The work was carried out as per your instructions.

**original** /əˈrɪdʒənl/（ep6）：
adj. 原先的，最早的，最初的
- The land was returned to its original owner.

**concern** /kənˈsɜːnɪŋ/（ep4）：
prep. 关于，有关，涉及
- calls from young children concerning lost pets

**prefer sb/sth to sb/sth**（ep4）：
比起…更喜欢…
- a child that prefers his imaginary world to reality

## 十、总体升级方法论

### 6.5 → 7.5 升级五大技法

| 技法 | 示例 | 来源 |
|------|------|------|
| Given倒装开头 | A graph...is given → Given is a graph... | ep2 |
| With独立主格 | 句号变逗号 + 动词变ing + 主语前加with | ep2 |
| Although趋势冲突 | 前后时间变化不一致时用 | ep2 |
| until + 时间点 | no one took part in it until 2005 | ep2 |
| What percentage变形 | 百分比+名词 → what percentage + 名词 + from/in | ep3 |

### 7.5 → 9 升级三大技法

| 技法 | 示例 | 来源 |
|------|------|------|
| 无痕衔接 | Having done / 分词形容词 / from which | ep9 |
| 省略性定语从句 | people who played → people playing | ep2 |
| 高级词汇替换 | the lion's share / engaged in / ranked last | ep2,4 |

### 6.5+ 核心句式模板库

- 头尾相接：`The figure for A increased from X to Y between [year1] and [year2]`
- 时间表达：`over the first five years` / `over the next decade` / `throughout the period`
- 过渡词：`commencing with` / `with regard to` / `concerning` / `as for` / `when it comes to`
- 显性衔接：`the first step is that` / `and then` / `after which` / `the final step is`
