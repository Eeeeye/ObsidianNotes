## 长难句

| 原句 | 结构拆分 | 译文 | 备注 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| But such transits have paved the way for what might prove to be one of the most vital breakthroughs in the cosmos - detecting Earth-sized planets orbiting other stars. | 主干：such transits have paved the way for ...；`what might prove to be ...` 表示“可能被证明是……”；破折号后是对 breakthrough 的具体解释。 | 但这种凌日现象为一项可能成为宇宙研究中最重要突破之一的事情铺平了道路，也就是发现围绕其他恒星运行的地球大小行星。 | 重点记 `pave the way for` 和 `what might prove to be` |
| But there was a problem: transits of Venus, unlike those of Mercury, are rare, occurring in pairs roughly eight years apart every hundred or so years. | 主干：there was a problem；transits of Venus are rare；`unlike those of Mercury` 作比较插入；`occurring in pairs ...` 补充说明 rarity。 | 但有一个问题：金星凌日和水星凌日不同，它非常罕见，通常每隔一百多年才会成对出现一次，而这一对之间大约相隔八年。 | 重点记 `unlike` 和 `occur in pairs` |
| They were based at a girls' school, where - it is alleged - the combined forces of three schoolmistresses outperformed the professionals with the accuracy of their observations. | 主干：They were based at a girls' school；定语从句：where ... outperformed ...；`it is alleged` 是插入语；`with the accuracy of their observations` 表示“在观测准确性方面”。 | 他们驻扎在一所女子学校，据说在那里，三位女教师合力进行的观测，在准确性上超过了专业人员。 | 重点记 `where` 定语从句和插入语 |
