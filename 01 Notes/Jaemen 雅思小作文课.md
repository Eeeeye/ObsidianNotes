## 一、全局框架

### 写作结构

```
Introduction → Overview（2-3点）→ 主体段（2-3段）
```

### 核心分类体系（第5集）

**不要按图表外观分类**（线形图/柱状图/饼图/表格）
**应按数据逻辑分类**：

| 分类 | 判断标准 | 代表题型 |
|------|---------|---------|
| 动态图 | X轴为连续时间，有趋势变化 | 线形图为代表 |
| 不连续时间 | 2-3个时间点，需抓方向+动静结合 | 柱状图常见 |
| 静态图 | 无时间轴，比较不同类别 | 饼图/柱状图 |
| 流程图 | 步骤/工序描述 | Diagram |
| 地图题 | 空间布局变化 | Map |

### 备考原则

1. **先拆解后组装**：早期练习各部分分开写，不要追求一气呵成
2. **背诵优先**：前期必须背熟基本表达和框架
3. **抓大放小**：整体完成度 > 细节完美
4. **时态**：过去时间用过去式，无标注用一般现在时

## 二、第1集：线性图入门「6.5+」（22:19）

### Introduction（引入段）

从题目提取三个信息：数据内容、国家/类别、时间段。

**模板**：
```
The graph illustrates how the [数据内容] changed in [国家] over a [时间段]
```

**变形记技巧**：
- 数据内容用 `how...changed` 转换
- 数量超过3个时说 `in four countries`
- 时间段用连字符：`a 40-year period`（连字符前后无空格、不加s）

### Overview（概述段）

2-3 句话概括整体趋势。核心方法：**头尾相接**（只看头尾变化）。

```
Overall, it is clear that...
The amount of X in A and B showed a dramatic increase.
While the figure for C and D showed a downward trend.
```

关注最大值：`the UK produces the largest amount of...`

**关键词**：upward trend, downward trend, figure

### 主体段

- 常见 2-3 段，小作文总体 4-5 段
- 分段逻辑：上升的一组、下降的一组

### 描述变化的三种方式

| 方式 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 动词+副词 | V+adv | increased significantly / decreased slightly |
| 名词+形容词 | showed a + adj + n | showed a dramatic growth / experienced a small drop |
| 高级词 | 单动词 | soared（激增）/ plummeted（骤降）/ levelled off（持平） |

### 时间表达

| 阶段 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 前期 | over the first five years / over the first decade |
| 中期 | over the next [time] |
| 后期 | over the last five years |
| 时间点 | at the beginning/end of the period / by the end of... |
| 全程 | throughout the period |

### 多段变化处理

- 连接词：`and then` / `after which`（定语从句直接衔接）
- 三段变化用"二二一"分割
- 结尾可用过去完成时：`by 2077, it had increased to six`（7-7.5）

### 大小等超（比较技巧）

| 类型 | 处理方式 |
|------|---------|
| 大 | 数值比较（最大值） |
| 小 | 最小值 |
| 等 | 数值相等 → `equaling that for [国家] in [年份]` |
| 超 | 后来居上 → `outnumbering that for [国家]` |

### 段落过渡

- 段内过渡：`A similar trend can also be found in Sweden` / `The figure for Sweden also declined, but more significantly`
- 段间过渡：`by contrast` / `similarly`
- 信号词：`commencing with [第一条线]`

## 三、第2集：线性图上天「7.5+」（14:05）

### 高级 Introduction：Given 倒装句

```
基本：A graph illustrating... is given（头重脚轻 ✗）
倒装：Given is a graph illustrating...（直接背诵 ✓）
```

### 灵活改写：用"流行度"替代"数量"

| 原始 | 改写 |
|------|------|
| 人数增加 | The popularity of X had grown significantly |
| 人数减少 | X became less attractive |
| 最大值 | the most popular activity throughout the period |

### With 引导的独立主格（7.5-8分）

**操作步骤**：①句号变逗号 ②动词变 ing ③主语前加 with

```
原句：the UK produced the largest amount
→ with the UK producing the largest amount
```

### Fluctuate 的两种用法

1. `fluctuated between A and B`（标注波动上下限）
2. `fluctuated around X`（标注中间值）

上升/下降但有波动：加小尾巴 `with minor fluctuation`

### 高级表达

- `accounted for the lion's share` — 占了最大份额
- `people engaged in...` — 参与...的人
- 省略性定语从句：`people who played → people playing`（7-7.5）
- `no one took part in it until 2005`
- `Although` + 前后趋势冲突时使用

## 四、第3集：饼状图入门「6.5+」（15:05）

### 静态图核心

- 无时间轴 → 无随时间变化
- 时态：**一般现在时**
- 出现形式：饼图、柱状图均可

### Introduction 改写

```
动态图：how the number changed
静态图：how...differ（用动词 differ）
```

**模板**：
```
Given are charts showing how the average percentages of three different nutrients differ in four different meals
```

### 高级 Introduction：What percentage 句式

```
基本：42% of added sugar is consumed in snacks
转换：What percentage of three nutrients people consume from four different meals
```

规则：百分比+名词 → what percentage，保留 from/in 结构

### Overview

关注**最大、最小、共同点**。3 个信息点 + while/with 框架连接。

题目暗示利用：最低值 → 最健康（`being the healthiest meal`）

### 主体段：大小等倍

| 关系 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 大 | 两个数字差别大 |
| 小 | 差别小 |
| 等 | `A equals B` / `A and B are the same, with each accounting for X%` |
| 倍 | `twice as high as` / `three times as high as` |

### 关键表达

- 对比：`as opposed to`（省略性定语从句）、`whereas`
- 小差距：`slightly higher than...` / `the next is...`
- 过渡词：`commencing with` / `with regard to` / `concerning`

**百分比语法**：单复数取决于后面的名词（people 是复数 → 复数动词）

## 五、第4集：柱状图上天「7.5+」（13:41）

### 分组策略

- **推荐**：按图表外的图例分组（三个选项 → 三个主体段）
- 替代：按高中低顺序、按相似趋势分

### Overview 升级

不仅描述数据 → 转为**习惯/偏好**描述：

```
going to a cafe was the most common habit
with people generally preferring instant coffee to fresh coffee
```

### 主体段内部再分组

- 五个数据 → 二三或二二一
- 优先高中低逻辑顺序
- 三个高于 60 → 三二分组

### 高级表达

- `choice` / `category` 替代类别名
- `residents` / `citizens` 替代 people
- `did so` 指代前文行为
- `ranked last was...`（倒装句，最后一名专用）
- `approximately` / `nearly` / `just more than`

### 过渡连接

- `with regard to` / `concerning` / `as for` / `when it comes to`
- `compared with` 承上启下

## 七、第6集：地图题入门「6.5+」（20:47）

### Introduction 模板

```
当前布局：Given are two maps illustrating the current layout and changes that will be introduced
过去时间：the layout of X in 2007 and changes that had been introduced
```

万能词：**layout**（适用于所有地图题）

### Overview：目的+细节

```
整体目的：renovation will enable X to better cater for [人群]
细节：with the introduction of...（增加和扩大的建筑）
```

核心逻辑：装修花钱 → 服务于人

### 动静变化四要素：新旧大小

| 变化 | 动词 | 名词 | 注意 |
|------|------|------|------|
| 新（增加） | construct / introduce | construction / introduction | — |
| 旧（拆除） | demolish → make way for | demolition | 腾出空间 |
| 大（扩大） | enlarge / expand | expansion | eastwards 向东 |
| 小（缩小） | be reduced / made smaller | reduction | — |

### 方位描述

| 位置关系 | 表达 |
|----------|------|
| 在内部 | in the north/south/east/west of |
| 紧邻 | on the left/right hand side |
| 不相邻 | to the north/south/east of |
| 其他 | in front of / behind / next to / inside / outside / surrounded by |

### 空间顺序

- **优先**：中间→两边
- 箭头引导的空间视角（把自己当作参观者）

**倒装句**（7.5+ 语法，提升 CC 分数）：
```
In the middle is a building
To the east and west of it are two outdoor courts
```

### 变化描述

- `As per the redevelopment plan`（根据改造计划）
- 先整体 → 再两边 → 后中间
- `the area in the middle will remain unchanged except that...`
- **抓大放小**，不追求过于细节的描述
- `existing` / `original` 表示原有的

## 八、第7集：地图题上天「7.5+」（14:33）

### 两种流派

- **流派一**：按区域，变化前→变化后
- **流派二（推荐）**：先写变化前整体 → 再写变化后整体

### 分割逻辑：路是骨骼，建筑是肉

- 先介绍道路框架
- 再填充建筑变化
- 道路和建筑配套发展

### Overview 策略

- 从图例判断核心变化（工厂→住宅）
- 目的：让小镇更适合居住

```
This industrial estate will be transformed into a housing estate with construction of housing and community facilities
```

### 变化后描述：道路+设施二分

| 维度 | 内容 |
|------|------|
| 道路改进 | new mini roundabout, newly built roads, residential road, bridge |
| 设施配套 | shops, medical center, playground, school |
| 道路目的 | 解决进出问题、方便农业运输 |

### 高级表达

- `axis road` — 进入小镇的路
- `which brings convenience to...`
- `will be transformed into...`
- `are expected to be built`
- 过渡词：`likewise` / `correspondingly`

## 九、第8集：流程图基础「6.5+」（11:18）

### Introduction 三种方式

1. 直接用题目：`how...is manufactured/produced`
2. `the steps involved in the production of...`
3. **推荐**：`the diagram illustrates the process by which...are produced`

### Overview

- 数总步骤：`There are X steps`
- 两段式分割：制作流程 + 包装流程

```
The first stage is to make noodles, and then the second stage is to package them
```

### 动词三种分类

| 类型 | 来源 | 处理 |
|------|------|------|
| 题目给出 | 图片标注（mixer→mixed） | 直接使用 |
| 无提示 | 图片未给动词 | 用 `made into` 万能处理 |
| 名词提取 | cooking→cooked, drying→dried | 转被动 |

### 基本连接词

```
the first step is that...
and then...
after which...
the final step is...
```

### 段落引导

- `Commencing with the [first] stage`
- `With regard to the [second] stage`

### 润色技巧：地点定语从句

物质到达某地加工 → where 从句：

```
X is sent to a [machine], where it is [processed]
```

无具体名称的设备统一用 **machine**。

### 构词法：Hyphen 连接

- `noodle making stage` / `noodle packaging stage`
- 类似：`time-consuming`、`time-wasting`

## 十、第9集：流程图强化课「7.5+」（10:38）

### 分割逻辑

- 按物质状态变化（固体→液体→糖）
- 保证段落字数均衡（3:4 分割）

### Overview 升级

- `initial stage` 替代 first stage
- `later stage` 替代 second stage

### 自然衔接体系（CC 9分标准：without drawing attention）

**1. Having done 结构（完成时自然衔接）**
```
前句末：...to allow them to grow
下句首：Having grown for 12 to 18 months, they are harvested...
普通版：After growing for 12 to 18 months...
```

**2. 分词形容词衔接**
```
they are harvested
→ the harvested sugarcane（过去分词作形容词）
```

**3. Which 定语从句**
```
...is purified through a limestone filter
→ which makes it ready for the heating process
```

**4. Having been done 结构**
```
Having been heated in an evaporator for a period, the juice becomes syrup
```

**5. From which 结构（高级）**
```
基本：crystals are separated from the syrup in a centrifuge
高级：from which the sugar crystals are separated in a centrifuge
```

### 显性衔接 vs 隐性衔接

| 类型 | 示例 | 水平 |
|------|------|------|
| 显性 | the first step, after that, the final step | 6-7分 |
| 隐性/无痕 | having done, harvested, separated | 9分 |

段落过渡：`Next comes the sugar manufacturing stage`

### 字典条目

**fluctuate** /ˈflʌktʃueɪt/（ep2）：
- 动词：价格或数量波动、上下变动
- [+ between]：Prices were volatile, fluctuating between $20 and $40.
- [+ around]：The number of children fluctuates around 100.

**the lion's share (of sth)**（ep2）：
- （某物）最大的一份
- 例：The firm has captured the lion's share of the UK market.

**category** /ˈkætəɡɔːri/（ep4）：
- noun (pl. categories) — 类别、种类、范畴
- 例：There are five categories of workers.

**as per sth** — formal（ep6）：
- 按照某事物
- 例：The work was carried out as per your instructions.

**percentage of + 复数名词**（ep3, 语法提示）：
- 如果 a percentage of 后面的名词是复数，动词也要用复数
- 例：Only a small percentage of people are interested in politics.

### With 独立主格三模板（ep2）

PPT绿色高亮，直接背诵使用：

```
With its figure fluctuating around ..
With its figure increasing from A to B
With its figure remaining stable at ..
```

### Overview 三条线策略（ep3 饼状图）

针对多类别静态图：
1. 两个类别的共同最大来源 → The consumption of sodium and saturated fat is mainly from dinner
2. 一个类别的特殊模式 → Snacks tend to have more added sugar
3. 共同最小值 → Breakfast contains the lowest percentages of three nutrients

### 范文对比：普通 → 7.5+

**Overview 升级（ep2 墨尔本社交中心）**：

| 版本 | 范文 |
|------|------|
| 普通 | The number of participants in table tennis and musical performances showed an upward trend, while the figure for amateur dramatics showed a decline. The number of film club was the largest throughout the period |
| 7.5+ | The popularity of table tennis and musical performances had grown significantly while amateur dramatics became less attractive. Film club was the most popular activity throughout the period |

**Overview 升级（ep4 澳洲咖啡茶）**：

| 版本 | 范文 |
|------|------|
| 普通 | The percentage of people who went to a café for coffee or tea was the highest, except in Adelaide. The percentage of people who bought fresh coffee was the lowest in all cities |
| 7.5+ | Overall, it is clear that going to a café to have coffee or tea was the most common habit, except in Adelaide, with people generally preferring instant coffee to fresh coffee |

**修改技巧**：数量词 → 流行度词（popularity, common habit, prefer）；while/with 连接 Overview 的 3 个信息点。

### 省略性定语从句（PPT绿色高亮）

- `people who played table tennis` → `people playing table tennis`（ep2）
- `people who took part in...` → `people taking part in...`（ep2）
- `29% which is provided by lunch` → `29% from lunch`（ep3）
