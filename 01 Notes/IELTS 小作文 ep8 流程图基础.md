# IELTS 小作文 ep8 流程图基础「6.5+」（11:18）

> 剑桥15 Test 3 — 方便面制造流程  
> The diagram below shows how instant noodles are manufactured.

---

## 一、流程图 8 步（剑桥15 方便面）

| Step | English | 中文 |
|------|---------|------|
| 1 | Storage silos | 储存筒仓（面粉） |
| 2 | Mixer (water + oil added) | 搅拌器（加水+油） |
| 3 | Dough sheets (rollers) | 面片（滚压） |
| 4 | Dough strips (cutting) | 面条条（切割） |
| 5 | Noodles discs (mould) | 面饼（模具成型） |
| 6 | Cooking in oil + drying | 油炸烹饪 + 干燥 |
| 7 | Vegetables + spices added + cups | 添加蔬菜调料 + 装杯 |
| 8 | Labelling + sealing | 贴标签 + 封口 |

---

## 二、Introduction 三种写法

雅思流程图 Introduction 的核心任务：**改写题目**（paraphrase the rubric）。

| 类型 | 模板 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| ① how 结构 | The diagram illustrates **how** [product] **is manufactured**. | The diagram illustrates how instant noodles are manufactured. |
| ② steps 结构 | The diagram illustrates **the steps involved in** [doing sth]. | The diagram illustrates the steps involved in producing instant noodles. |
| ③ 推荐：process by which | The diagram illustrates **the process by which** [product] **are produced / made**. | The diagram illustrates the process by which instant noodles are produced. |

> **推荐第③种**：最自然、最地道，既覆盖了"流程"（process）又覆盖了"产品"（instant noodles），且没有重复题目原词。

---

## 三、Overview 写法

Overview 是 Task 1 的"定档段"——**必须有**，且要概括大阶段。

### 模板

```
Overall, there are [数字] steps in the process, 
from [起点原材料] to [最终成品]. 
The first stage is to [制造阶段], 
and then the second stage is to [包装阶段].
```

### 方便面范文 Overview

> Overall, there are eight steps in the process, from flour to the final instant noodles. The first stage is to make the noodles, and then the second stage is to package them.

核心技巧：把 8 步**分成两大阶段**——制作面条 vs 包装面条。

---

## 四、完整范文（Model Essay — Band 7+）

### 全文

The diagram illustrates the process by which instant noodles are produced.

Overall, there are eight steps in the process, from flour to the final instant noodles. The first stage is to make the noodles, and then the second stage is to package them.

**Commencing with the first stage**, flour is pumped from storage silos into a mixer, where it is combined with water and oil. The resulting mixture is then rolled into dough sheets. These sheets are subsequently cut into dough strips, which are then shaped into noodles discs in a mould. The discs are cooked in oil and then left to dry.

**With regard to the second stage**, the dried noodle discs are placed into cups, after which vegetables and spices are added. The final step is that the cups are labelled and sealed, ready for distribution.

（161 words）

---

### 范文逐句解析

#### Paragraph 1（制作流程 — 6 步合写）

| 句子 | 技巧 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| Commencing with the first stage, flour is pumped from storage silos into a mixer, **where** it is combined with water and oil. | where 地点定语从句 | "被送到搅拌器，在那里与水油混合" — 把两步合为一句 |
| The resulting mixture is then rolled into dough sheets. | made into 万能处理 | "混合物被滚压成面片" — roll → rolled |
| These sheets are subsequently cut into dough strips, **which** are then shaped into noodles discs in a mould. | which 定语从句 | "被切成面条条，然后在模具中成型为面饼" |
| The discs are cooked in oil and then left to dry. | 并列结构 | 烹饪 + 干燥两步合写 |

#### Paragraph 2（包装流程 — 2 步合写）

| 句子 | 技巧 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| With regard to the second stage, the dried noodle discs are placed into cups, **after which** vegetables and spices are added. | after which 连接 | "装入杯中，之后加入蔬菜和调料" |
| The final step is that the cups are labelled and sealed, ready for distribution. | ready for 结尾 | 用 ready for distribution 自然收尾 |

## 五、动词三种分类表

流程图最怕"没词可用"。记住三招提取动词：

| 分类 | 来源 | 示例 | 转换方式 |
|------|------|------|----------|
| **题目给出的动词** | 图上直接写的词 | pump → is pumped, mix → is mixed, cut → is cut | 直接用被动语态 be + done |
| **无提示 → made into** | 图上没有动词，只有名词/箭头 | dough sheets → is rolled into dough sheets; noodles discs → is shaped into noodles discs | 用 **is made into** 或具体动词 |
| **名词提取** | 把图上的名词变成动词 | cooking → is cooked; drying → is dried; labelling → is labelled; sealing → is sealed | 名词 + 被动语态 |

### 方便面例 — 全部 8 步的动词处理

| Step | 图上提示 | 范文用词 |
|------|----------|----------|
| Storage silos → Mixer | 箭头 + pump（间接） | **is pumped** into |
| Mixer | water + oil added | **is combined** with |
| Dough sheets | rollers | **is rolled** into |
| Dough strips | cutting | **is cut** into |
| Noodles discs | mould | **is shaped** into |
| Cooking + drying | 名词 cooking / drying | **is cooked** / **is left to dry** |
| Vegetables + spices | 名词 | **are added** |
| Labelling + sealing | 名词 labelling / sealing | **is labelled** / **is sealed** |

## 六、基本连接词（6.5+ 必备）

流程图最忌"裸连"——每一步之间必须有连接手段。

| 中文 | 英文 | 使用位置 |
|------|------|----------|
| 第一步是… | The first step is that... | 首步引导 |
| 然后… | and then... | 句中连接 |
| 随后… | after which... | 从句连接（非常推荐） |
| 最后一步是… | The final step is that... | 末步引导 |
| 接着… | Subsequently, ... / Next, ... | 句首连接 |

### 示例对照

| 低级连接（5.5 分） | 高级连接（6.5+ 分） |
|---------------------|----------------------|
| Flour goes to a mixer. Water and oil are added. It becomes dough sheets. | Flour is pumped into a mixer, **where** it is combined with water and oil. The resulting mixture **is then rolled into** dough sheets. |

## 七、段落引导词

流程图分段时，用引导词替代粗暴的 "The first stage is..."。

| 引导词 | 用法 | 示例 |
|--------|------|------|
| Commencing with the [first] stage, ... | 第一段开头 | Commencing with the first stage, flour is pumped... |
| With regard to the [second] stage, ... | 第二段开头 | With regard to the second stage, the dried noodles... |

## 八、润色技巧

### 1. where 从句（地点定语从句）

> 公式：**X is sent to a [machine], where it is [processed].**

- ❌ Flour goes to a mixer. In the mixer, it is combined with water and oil.  
- ✅ Flour is pumped into a mixer, **where** it is combined with water and oil.

### 2. hyphen 构词法（连字符造词）

在流程图中可以自造复合词来压缩表达、提升词汇丰富度。

| 构词 | 含义 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| noodle-making stage | 制面阶段 | The noodle-making stage involves six steps. |
| time-consuming | 耗时的 | Growing sugar cane is a time-consuming process. |
| ready-to-eat | 即食的 | The final product is a ready-to-eat meal. |

## 九、本集小结（Takeaways）

1. **Introduction**：用 "the process by which...are produced" 改写题目。
2. **Overview**：必须有 — 说清共几步 + 分成哪两大阶段。
3. **动词来源三招**：题目给 → 直接用被动；没提示 → made into；名词 → 提取动词。
4. **连接必用**：and then / after which / where / subsequently。
5. **分段引导词**：Commencing with... / With regard to...。
6. **润色两大法宝**：where 地点从句 + hyphen 构词法。
