# IELTS 小作文 ep4 柱状图上天「7.5+」（13:41）

**来源：** 剑桥雅思 15 Test 2  
**相关笔记：** [[Jaemen 雅思小作文课]]

---

## 题目

The chart below shows the results of a survey about people's coffee and tea buying and drinking habits in five Australian cities.

---

## 真实数据

| City | Bought fresh coffee (last 4 weeks) | Bought instant coffee (last 4 weeks) | Went to a café for coffee or tea (last 4 weeks) |
|------|-----------------------------------|--------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Sydney | 44 | 45 | 62 |
| Melbourne | 42 | 48 | 64 |
| Brisbane | 34 | 53 | 55 |
| Adelaide | 34 | 50 | 49（例外：唯一下降） |
| Hobart | 37 | 54 | 63 |

---

## Introduction

Given is a chart illustrating how Australians' coffee and tea buying and drinking habits differ in five areas.

> **要点：** 改写题目，不照抄原题。用 "Given is a chart illustrating..." 句式替代 "The chart shows..."。

---

## 分组策略

- **按图例分组** → 三个主体段（P1: café visits / P2: instant coffee / P3: fresh coffee）
- 五个城市数据 → 可采用 **2-2-1** 或 **2-3** 的结构
- 注意 Adelaide 是例外（café 数据低于其他两项），需要单独处理或对比

---

## Overview 升级

### 普通版（6分）
- The percentage of people who went to a café for coffee or tea was the highest, except in Adelaide
- The percentage of people who bought fresh coffee was the lowest in all cities

### 7.5+ 版
> Overall, it is clear that **going to a café** to have coffee or tea was the **most common habit**, except in Adelaide, **with people generally preferring instant coffee to fresh coffee**

> **升级要点：**
> - 补充 "among residents in all five cities" 使范围明确
> - 用 "except in..." 点出例外，体现数据对比意识
> - 加入 "where the opposite was true" 展现语言多样性

---

## 完整范文

### P1 — Café Visits（最高数据段）

Commencing with the choice of going to café for coffee or tea, more than 60% of people in Sydney, Melbourne, and Hobart did so in last four weeks. 55% of residents visited cafes in Brisbane and next is Adelaide, at nearly 50%.

> **要点：**
> - "did so" 替代重复 "went to a café"，避免用词单调
> - "approximately / nearly / just more than" 均为高级约数表达
> - Adelaide 数据最低（49%），单独放在句末处理

### P2 — Instant Coffee（中间数据段）

With regard to instant coffee, approximately 54% of people in Hobart bought instant coffee, slightly higher than that figure in Brisbane, at 53%. Next are Adelaide and Melbourne, at 50% and 48% respectively. **Ranked last was Sydney, at only nearly 45%**

> **要点：**
> - "With regard to" 是高级过渡连接词
> - "Ranked last was..." 倒装句式，7.5+ 标志
> - "ranged from...to..." 概括数据范围

### P3 — Fresh Coffee（最低数据段 + 对比）

Concerning fresh coffee, approximately 44% of people in Sydney had the habit of buying it, slightly higher than that figure in Melbourne, at 42%. Next is Hobart, at 37% and the percentages in Brisbane and Adelaide were the same, with each accounting for nearly 34%

Compared with instant coffee, fresh coffee was less popular. Approximately 44% of people in Sydney had the habit of buying it, slightly higher than that figure in Melbourne, at 42%. Next is Hobart, at 37% and the percentages in Brisbane and Adelaide are the same, with each accounting for nearly34%

> **要点：**
> - "When it comes to" 和 "Compared with" 均为高级过渡连接
> - "the trend was reversed" 体现对比分析
> - 用 "—" 补充解释，展示句式变化

---

## 高级表达总结

| 类别 | 表达 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 替代词 | choice / category | 替代 option / kind |
| 人群 | residents / citizens | 替代 people |
| 指代 | did so | 替代重复的动词短语 |
| 倒装 | Ranked last was... | 7.5+ 句式 |
| 约数 | approximately / nearly / just more than | 精准表达约数 |
| 例外 | the opposite was true / the trend was reversed | 对比数据 |

---

## 过渡连接词

| 功能 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 关于... | with regard to |
| 关于... | concerning |
| 关于... | as for |
| 当谈到... | when it comes to |
| 与...相比 | compared with |

---

## 字典（发音 & 语法）

| 词 | 音标 | 语法点 |
|----|------|--------|
| category | /ˈkætəɡɔːri/ | 名词，复数 categories |
| concerning | /kənˈsɜːnɪŋ/ | 介词 = about，非 verb |
| prefer A to B | — | **介词用 to，不是 than** |

> **语法注意：** prefer doing A to doing B / prefer A to B  
> 错误：~~prefer coffee than tea~~  
> 正确：prefer coffee **to** tea

## 时态注意

- 图表描述用 **一般现在时**（除非题目注明特定年份）
- 本题目数据为 survey results，全文使用现在时：
  - "is the most popular choice"
  - "figures range from..."
  - "the trend is reversed"
- overview 用 "was the most common habit" 可以（因描述的是 survey 结果，过去式亦可），保持一致即可
