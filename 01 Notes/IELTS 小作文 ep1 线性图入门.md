# IELTS 小作文 ep1 线性图入门「6.5+」（22:19）

> Jaemen阿古门特 | 题型：线形图 | 等级：6.5+
> 回到：[[Jaemen 雅思小作文课]]

---

## 题目

> The graph below shows average carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions per person in the United Kingdom, Sweden, Italy and Portugal between 1967 and 2007.

### 真实数据

| 国家 | 趋势 | 数据 |
|------|------|------|
| UK | 缓慢下降，全程最高 | 11→10→9吨 |
| Sweden | 先升后降 | 9→10(1977峰值)→7→5吨 |
| Italy | 先升后稳 | 4→6→7.5→7.5吨 |
| Portugal | 持续上升至持平 | 1.5→5→5吨 |

---

## Introduction 改写

**模板**：
```
The graph illustrates how the [数据内容] changed in [国家] over a [时间段]
```

**变形记示范**：
```
Given is a graph illustrating the production of four kinds of vehicles in the United Kingdom from 1995 to 2005.
→ A graph illustrating how the production...changed over a 10-year period...is given.
```

要点：数据用 `how...changed` 转换 | 数量>3 用 `in four countries` | 时间段连字符 `a 40-year period`（无空格、不加s）

---

## Overview

核心方法：**头尾相接**（只看头尾变化）

> Overall, it is clear that the amount of CO2 in Italy and Portugal showed a dramatic increase, while the figures for Sweden and the UK showed a downward trend. The UK produced the largest amount of CO2 throughout the period.

关键词：upward trend, downward trend, figure

---

## 完整范文（4段）

**P1 — UK（最高值）**：
> Commencing with the UK, the figure for it almost stayed unchanged over the first decade at nearly 11 tons, after which it steadily declined to about 9 tons in 2007. Despite this decline, the average carbon dioxide emissions in the UK were still the largest among the four countries throughout the period.

**P2 — Sweden（段内过渡）**：
> The figure for Sweden increased from 9 tons in 1967 to 10 tons, and then it declined significantly to 7 tons in 1987. Over the next two decades, it slightly decreased to nearly 5 tons.

**P3 — Italy + Portugal（段间过渡）**：
> By contrast, the figure for Italy increased from just more than 4 tons in 1967 to more than 6 tons in 1977, and then it grew slightly to nearly 7.5 tons in 1997, outnumbering that for Sweden in 1990. Over the next decade, it had levelled off. Similarly, the figure for Portugal increased from approximately 1.5 tons to 5 tons over the first three decades, after which it remained unchanged, equaling that for Sweden in 2007.

---

## 描述变化的三种方式

| 方式 | 结构 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| 动词+副词 | V+adv | increased significantly / decreased slightly |
| 名词+形容词 | showed a + adj + n | showed a dramatic growth / experienced a small drop |
| 高级词 | 单动词 | soared / plummeted / levelled off |

## 时间表达

| 阶段 | 表达 |
|------|------|
| 前期 | over the first five years / over the first decade |
| 中期 | over the next [time] |
| 后期 | over the last five years |
| 时间点 | at the beginning/end of the period |
| 全程 | throughout the period |

## 大小等超

| 类型 | 处理 |
|------|------|
| 大 | 最大值 |
| 小 | 最小值 |
| 等 | `equaling that for [国家] in [年份]` |
| 超 | `outnumbering that for [国家]` |

## 段落过渡

- 段内：`A similar trend can also be found in...` / `The figure for X also declined, but more significantly`
- 段间：`by contrast` / `similarly`
- 信号词：`commencing with [第一条线]`

## 多段变化

- 连接词：`and then` / `after which`
- 三段变化用"二二一"分割
- 结尾可用过去完成时：`by 2077, it had increased to six`（7-7.5）
