# IELTS 小作文 ep2 线性图上天「7.5+」（14:05）

> 来源：[[Jaemen 雅思小作文课]]

---

## 题目

The graph below gives information on the numbers of participants for different activities at one social centre in Melbourne, Australia for the period 2000 to 2020.

---

## 真实数据 Table

| Activity | 趋势 | 数据描述 |
|---|---|---|
| Film club | 稳定高位 | 60 → 65（最高，在 2010 年左右达到峰值 65） |
| Martial arts | 稳定 | 35 → 40，全程波动很小，始终在 35-40 区间 |
| Amateur dramatics | 大幅下降 | 25 → 5，持续下滑 |
| Table tennis | 大幅上升 | 15 → 50，持续增长，后期超越 martial arts |
| Musical performances | 从零起步 | 0 → 15（2005 年后出现，缓慢增长） |

---

## Given 倒装句

普通写法：
> A graph is given that shows the numbers of participants...

倒装升级（7.5+）：
> Given is a graph illustrating participant numbers across five different activities at a social centre in Melbourne, Australia, spanning the period from 2000 to 2020.

用法说明：将过去分词（Given / Shown / Depicted / Presented）提前至句首 + is + 主语，形成倒装结构，语气更正式、更具学术色彩。

---

## 流行度改写

避免重复使用「the number of people increased/decreased」，改用 popularity 视角：

| 原意 | 改写 |
|---|---|
| 人数增加 | The popularity of table tennis had grown significantly. |
| 人数减少 | Amateur dramatics became far less attractive over the period. |
| 数值最大 | Film club was the most popular activity throughout. |
| 数据稳定 | Martial arts maintained a steady level of popularity. |
| 从无到有 | Musical performances gradually gained in popularity from 2005 onwards. |

关键句式模板：
- The popularity of [A] grew considerably over the [N]-year period.
- [B] became increasingly less popular, with its participation figure plummeting from X to Y.
- [C] remained the most popular activity, never being overtaken.

---

## Overview 三级批改对比

### 普通版（6.0）
> Overall, film club had the most participants. Table tennis increased, and amateur dramatics decreased. Musical performances had the lowest numbers.

问题：堆砌事实，无分析层次，句式单调，缺少数据概括。

### 7.5+ 版
> Overall, film club consistently attracted the highest number of participants, while table tennis experienced a dramatic rise in popularity, becoming the second most popular activity by the end. In contrast, amateur dramatics saw a sharp decline, and musical performances, despite appearing later, remained the least popular throughout.

优点：有对比逻辑（while / in contrast），有概括（consistently / dramatic / sharp），提及时间线与排名变化。

### ⭐ 5星版（With 独立主格）
> Overall, film club was by far the most popular activity throughout the period, with its participation figure fluctuating around 60–65 and consistently dominating all other activities. Table tennis gained remarkable momentum, with its numbers rising from 15 to 50, ultimately overtaking martial arts. By contrast, amateur dramatics lost its appeal, with the figure falling sharply from 25 to 5, while musical performances, with their numbers hovering below 15, remained the least popular option.

升级点：
- 每个活动都使用了「,with + 独立主格」对数据进行二次概括
- 逻辑层次清晰：最高 | 上升超越 | 下降 | 低位的四层结构
- 词汇丰富：by far / gained momentum / overtaking / lost its appeal / hovering below

---

## With 独立主格三模板

独立主格 = 句子, with + 名词/代词 + 动词-ing/过去分词/介词短语

此结构在 Task 1 中用于**在一个句子里既给出趋势判断又给出数据支撑**，是 7.5+ 的核心句型。

### 模板一：波动 → with its figure fluctuating around X
> Film club was the most popular activity, **with its figure fluctuating around 60 to 65 members** over the two decades.

可替换词：hovering around / oscillating between / remaining in the range of

### 模板二：上升/下降 → with its number increasing from A to B
> Table tennis saw the most dramatic growth, **with the number of participants increasing from just 15 in 2000 to 50 by 2020**.

可替换词：rising from A to B / climbing steadily / surging / growing significantly

### 模板三：稳定 → with its figure remaining stable at X
> Martial arts maintained a consistent following, **with its figure remaining stable at approximately 35 to 40 participants** throughout the entire period.

可替换词：staying constant at / holding steady at / showing little variation around

## 完整范文 4 段

### Paragraph 1 — Introduction（倒装 + 改写）

> Given is a graph illustrating how the number of participants for different activities changed at one social center in Melbourne, Australia over a 20-year period from 2000 to 2020.

亮点：
- Given is...倒装开篇
- how...changed 动态图改写
- one social center in Melbourne, Australia over a 20-year period（地点+时间段）

---

### Paragraph 2 — Body 1（Film club + Martial arts，lion's share 版）

> Commencing with the film club, participants in it accounted for the lion's share, with its number fluctuating between 60 and 65. A similar stable trend can be found in martial arts: approximately 35 people engaged in this activity in 2000, and the number fluctuated around 35 people.

亮点：
- lion's share = 最大份额（高级表达）
- engaged in = who were engaged in（省略性定语从句）

---

### Paragraph 3 — Body 2（Table tennis + Musical performances + Although 升级）

> With regard to table tennis, although the number of people interested in it remained almost unchanged at 15 over the first decade, it soared to 50 in 2020. Similarly, the popularity of musical performances also grew: Although no one took part in it until 2005, the number of participants had steadily increased to nearly 15 by 2020 since 2005.

亮点：
- Although... 升级对比（前后两段时间变化不一致时使用）
- 省略性定语从句：people interested in it = people who were interested in it

---

### Paragraph 4 — By contrast（Amateur dramatics + with 独立主格收尾）

> By contrast, fewer people chose amateur dramatics during this period. Although the number of participants slightly increased from 25 to 28 over the first five years, it had steadily declined to only 5 by the end of the period, with this activity being the least popular in 2020.

亮点：
- with this activity being the least popular in 2020（With独立主格收尾）

## Fluctuate 两种用法

| 用法 | 结构 | 例句 |
|---|---|---|
| 在 A 与 B 之间波动 | fluctuate **between** A **and** B | The figure for martial arts fluctuated between 35 and 40. |
| 在某个数值附近波动 | fluctuate **around** X | Film club participation fluctuated around 60 to 65 members. |

注意：fluctuate 暗示有升有降、非单向的变化，因此不可用于持续上升或持续下降的数据线（如 table tennis 的 15→50 就不能用 fluctuate）。

---

## 高级表达速查

| 表达 | 含义 | 使用场景 |
|---|---|---|
| lion's share | 最大份额 | 描述占比最大的类别 |
| commanded the lion's share | 占据了最大份额 | Task 1 最高数据线 |
| engaged in | 参与（= took part in） | 替代 participated in |
| underwent a remarkable surge | 经历了显著激增 | 大幅上升的线 |
| a stark reversal of fortunes | 命运急剧逆转 | 对比两条相反趋势线 |
| declined precipitously | 急剧下降 | 替代 decreased sharply |
| occupied the bottom rung | 处于最末位置 | 描述最低的数据线 |
| albeit | 尽管（比 although 更简洁正式） | 让步状语 |
| by a considerable margin | 以相当大的差距 | 拉开差距 |
| dwarfed all competing activities | 使所有其他活动相形见绌 | 最高数据线遥遥领先 |

## 字典

### fluctuate
**/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/** — verb

1. （数量、数值等）波动，上下起伏
   - *to rise and fall irregularly in number or amount*
   - 例句：Oil prices fluctuated significantly during the crisis.
   - IELTS 用法：The figure fluctuated between 35 and 40 throughout the period.

2. 近义词：vacillate, oscillate, vary, swing
3. 派生词：fluctuation (n.) / fluctuating (adj.)
4. 搭配：fluctuate wildly（剧烈波动）/ fluctuate slightly（小幅波动）/ fluctuate around X / fluctuate between A and B

---

### the lion's share
**/ðə ˈlaɪənz ʃeə(r)/** — noun phrase (idiom)

1. 最大份额；绝大部分
   - *the largest part or portion of something*
   - 语源：出自《伊索寓言》，狮子与其他动物一起捕猎后独占全部猎物。
   - IELTS 用法：Film club commanded the lion's share of participants.
   - 例句：The lion's share of the budget was allocated to infrastructure.

2. 近义表达：the bulk of / the vast majority of / the predominant portion of
3. 注意：lion's share 暗示一个类别占比远超其他，用于有显著优势的数据线。

## 省略性定语从句

普通定语从句（6.0 水平）：
> the number of people **who played** table tennis

省略性定语从句（7.5+ 水平）：
> the number of people **playing** table tennis

规则：当关系代词（who / which / that）在定语从句中作**主语**，且从句动词为**主动语态**时，可将「关系代词 + be 动词」一并省略，或将「关系代词 + 主动动词」中的关系代词省去，动词改为现在分词。

更多例子：

| 原句 | 省略后 |
|---|---|
| participants who were engaged in martial arts | participants engaged in martial arts |
| members who took part in film club | members taking part in film club |
| the figure which was recorded in 2000 | the figure recorded in 2000 |
| the trend that dominated the chart | the trend dominating the chart |

Task 1 高频应用：在描述 participants / members / people / those 时大量使用此技巧。
