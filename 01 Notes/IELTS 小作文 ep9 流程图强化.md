# IELTS 小作文 ep9 流程图强化「7.5+」（10:38）

> 剑桥16 Test 2 — 甘蔗制糖流程  
> The diagram below shows how sugar is produced from sugar cane.

---

## 一、流程图 7 步（剑桥16 甘蔗制糖）

### 阶段划分：固体 → 液体 → 糖

| 阶段 | Step | English | 中文 |
|------|------|---------|------|
| **固体阶段** | 1 | Growing (12–18 months) | 种植（12–18 个月） |
| | 2 | Harvesting (manual / machine) | 收割（人工 / 机械） |
| **液体阶段** | 3 | Crushing → juice | 压碎 → 出汁 |
| | 4 | Purifying (limestone filter) | 净化（石灰过滤） |
| | 5 | Evaporator → syrup (加热蒸发) | 蒸发器 → 糖浆 |
| **糖阶段** | 6 | Centrifuge (分离糖晶) | 离心机（分离糖晶体） |
| | 7 | Drying + cooling | 干燥 + 冷却 |

---

## 二、Introduction 写法（7.5+ 升级版）

在 ep8 的基础上，ep9 直接给出高分 Introduction：

> **Given is a diagram illustrating the process by which sugar is manufactured from sugar cane.**

### 升级点分析

| 基础版（6.5） | 升级版（7.5+） |
|---------------|-----------------|
| The diagram illustrates... | **Given is** a diagram illustrating... |
| the process by which sugar is produced | the process by which sugar **is manufactured** from sugar cane |

> **Given is** 倒装开头 = 直接告诉考官"我会写高级句式"。

---

## 三、Overview 写法（用 initial / later 替代 first / second）

ep8 用了 first stage / second stage，ep9 升级为 **initial stage / later stage**。

### 范文 Overview

> Overall, there are seven steps in this process, from growing sugar cane to the final sugar product. The initial stage is to collect sugar cane to produce juice, and the later stage is to make sugar from the juice.

### 升级点分析

| 基础版（6.5） | 升级版（7.5+） |
|---------------|-----------------|
| first stage / second stage | **initial stage** / **later stage** |
| make noodles → package noodles | collect sugar cane to produce juice → **make sugar from the juice** |

> **核心思维**：Overview 不数步骤个数，而是说出"大逻辑"——先取汁，后制糖。

## 四、完整范文（Model Essay — Band 8+）

### 全文

**Given is a diagram illustrating the process by which the sugar is manufactured from sugar cane.**

**Overall, there are seven steps from sugar cane to sugar. The initial stage is to collect sugar cane to produce juice, and the later stage is to make sugar.**

**Commencing with the juice-producing stage, the first step is to plant sugar canes to allow them to grow. Having grown for 12-18 months, they are harvested either by industrial farming machines or by hand. After that, the harvested sugar canes are then sent to a crushing machine, where they are crushed to produce raw sugar cane juice.**

**Next comes the manufacturing stage.** The juice is purified through a limestone filter, which makes it ready for the heating process. Having been heated in an evaporator for a period, the juice becomes syrup, from which the sugar crystals are separated in a centrifuge. The final step is to dry and cool the separated sugar crystals, after which sugar is made.

（160 words）

---

### 范文逐句深度解析

#### Paragraph 1（juice producing stage — 取汁阶段，3:4 分割）

| # | 句子 | 衔接技巧 | 详解 |
|---|------|----------|------|
| ① | Commencing with the juice producing stage, sugar cane is first grown for 12 to 18 months. | 段落引导词 + hyphen 构词 | juice producing stage = 自造复合名词 |
| ② | **Having matured**, the sugar cane is then harvested, either manually or by machine. | **Having done** 分词完成式 | = After it has matured → 无痕衔接，省略主语和时间连词 |
| ③ | **The harvested sugar cane** is subsequently crushed, producing juice. | **分词形容词** + 现在分词结果状语 | harvested 作前置定语; producing juice 作结果状语 |
| ④ | After that, the juice is purified by being passed through a limestone filter. | by being done 方式状语 | 把 purify 的"手段"写出来 |
| ⑤ | **Having been purified**, the juice is then heated in an evaporator, **where** it is transformed into syrup. | **Having been done** + **where 地点从句** | 双重衔接——前用完成被动分词衔接上句，后用 where 从句展开本句 |

#### Paragraph 2（sugar manufacturing stage — 制糖阶段）

| # | 句子 | 衔接技巧 | 详解 |
|---|------|----------|------|
| ⑥ | With regard to the sugar manufacturing stage, the syrup is placed into a centrifuge, **from which** sugar crystals are separated. | **from which** 结构 | = and from the centrifuge sugar crystals are separated → 用 from which 合一 |
| ⑦ | **Next comes** the drying and cooling process, which turns the crystals into the final sugar product. | **Next comes 倒装** + which 定语从句 | Next comes + 主语（全部倒装）= 高级替代 "The next step is..." |

## 五、6 种自然衔接技巧（7.5+ 核心）

ep9 的重点不再是"能不能连起来"，而是"连得自然不自然、高级不高级"。

### 技巧清单

| # | 技巧名称 | 公式 | 范文例句 |
|---|----------|------|----------|
| ① | **Having done**（分词完成式） | Having + 过去分词, 主句 | **Having matured**, the sugar cane is then harvested. |
| ② | **分词形容词作前置定语** | the + 过去分词 + 名词 | **The harvested sugar cane** is subsequently crushed. |
| ③ | **which 定语从句** | 主句, which + V | ...heated in an evaporator, **which** turns it into syrup. |
| ④ | **Having been done**（完成被动分词） | Having been + 过去分词, 主句 | **Having been purified**, the juice is then heated. |
| ⑤ | **from which 结构** | 主句, from which + 主语 + V | ...placed into a centrifuge, **from which** sugar crystals are separated. |
| ⑥ | **Next comes 倒装** | Next comes + 主语 | **Next comes** the drying and cooling process. |

## 六、显性衔接 vs 隐性衔接对比表

| 维度 | 显性衔接（6.5） | 隐性衔接（7.5+） |
|------|------------------|-------------------|
| 时间连词 | After that, the juice... | **Having been purified**, the juice... |
| 主语重复 | The sugar cane is harvested. Then the sugar cane is crushed. | **The harvested sugar cane** is subsequently crushed. |
| 步骤引导 | The next step is to dry and cool the sugar. | **Next comes** the drying and cooling process. |
| 地点转换 | The syrup goes to a centrifuge. Sugar crystals are separated. | The syrup is placed into a centrifuge, **from which** sugar crystals are separated. |
| 效果 | 能得 6.5，但显生硬 | 自然流畅，可达 7.5+ |

### 核心原则

> **显性衔接靠"连接词"**（and then, after that, next...）  
> **隐性衔接靠"语法结构"**（分词、定语从句、倒装、from which）

---

## 七、7.5 → 9 升级三大技法

| 技法 | 6.5 写法 | 7.5+ 升级 | 9 分极致 | 说明 |
|------|----------|-----------|----------|------|
| **分词衔接** | After the sugar cane is harvested, it is crushed. | **Harvested**, the sugar cane is crushed. | **Having been harvested**, the sugar cane is crushed. | 越简洁越高级；Having been done 是最完整的隐性衔接 |
| **地点从句** | The juice goes to an evaporator. Then it becomes syrup. | The juice is heated in an evaporator, **where** it becomes syrup. | The juice is heated in an evaporator, **where** it is **transformed into** syrup. | 替换 become 为 transform into = 词汇升级 |
| **倒装引导** | The final step is the drying and cooling process. | The final step involves drying and cooling. | **Next comes** the drying and cooling process, **from which** the finished sugar is obtained. | 倒装 + from which 连环衔接 |

## 九、两种流程图的通用思维框架

ep8 和 ep9 合在一起，构成雅思流程图的**完整应对体系**：

### 思维框架

```
[Introduction]  改写题目 → the process by which...are produced / manufactured
[Overview]      总步数 + 两大阶段（initial stage / later stage）
[Body P1]       第一阶段：Starting with... / Commencing with...
                用 6 大衔接技巧把 3-5 步串成一段
[Body P2]       第二阶段：With regard to the later stage...
                用 6 大衔接技巧把 2-4 步串成一段
```

### 方便面 vs 甘蔗制糖对比

| 维度 | ep8 方便面 | ep9 甘蔗制糖 |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| 来源 | 剑桥 15 | 剑桥 16 |
| 步数 | 8 步 | 7 步 |
| 阶段 | 制面 + 包装 | 取汁 + 制糖 |
| 衔接水平 | 基础连接词（and then, after which, where） | 高级衔接（Having done, Having been done, from which, Next comes） |

| 核心技能 | 动词提取三招 + 基础连接 | 隐性衔接六技巧 + 词汇升级 |

## 十、本集小结（Takeaways）

1. **Introduction 升级**：用 **Given is...** 倒装开头。
2. **Overview 升级**：用 **initial stage / later stage** 替代 first / second。
3. **六大衔接技巧**：Having done / 分词形容词 / which 从句 / Having been done / from which / Next comes 倒装。
4. **隐性 > 显性**：用语法结构衔接，而非堆砌连接词。
5. **词汇升级**：manufacture, transform into, be obtained, subsequent stage。
6. **3:4 分割法**：7 步流程，前 3 步取汁（含种植收割），后 4 步制糖——自然分段。
