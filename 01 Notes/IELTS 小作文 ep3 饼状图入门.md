# IELTS 小作文 ep3 饼状图入门「6.5+」（15:05）

> Jaemen阿古门特 | 题型：饼状图 | 等级：6.5+
> 回到：[[Jaemen 雅思小作文课]]

---

## 题目

> The charts below show the average percentages in typical meals of three types of nutrients, all of which may be harmful if eaten too much.

三个饼图分别展示：Sodium（钠）、Saturated fat（饱和脂肪）、Added sugar（添加糖）在典型 meals 中的平均百分比分布。

### 真实数据

| 营养素 | Breakfast | Lunch | Dinner | Snacks |
|--------|-----------|-------|--------|--------|
| **Sodium** | 14% | 29% | **43%（最大）** | 14% |
| **Saturated fat** | 16% | 26% | **37%（最大）** | 21% |
| **Added sugar** | 16% | 23% | 19% | **42%（最大）** |

> 关键观察：Sodium 和 Saturated fat 的 dinner 占比最大；Added sugar 的 snacks 占比最大。三者 breakfast 均为最小值或接近最小值。

---

## 静态图 vs 动态图

| 对比维度 | 动态图（线形图） | 静态图（饼状图） |
|----------|------------------|------------------|
| 核心特征 | 有时间轴 | 无时间轴 |
| 时态 | 一般过去时 | **一般现在时** |
| 核心问题 | 数据如何**变化** | 数据如何**不同** |
| 信号词 | increased, declined, over the period | is the largest, accounts for, differs |

> 饼状图 = 静态图 = 无时间轴 → **全程使用一般现在时**

---

## Introduction 改写

**动态图模板**：
```
The graph illustrates how...changed in...over a period...
```

**静态图模板**：
```
Given are charts showing how...differ in...
```

**本题目变形记示范**：

- 原题：`The charts below show the average percentages in typical meals of three types of nutrients`
- 改写：`Given are three pie charts illustrating how the average percentages of three nutrients differ in typical meals in the United States.`

要点：
- `show` → `illustrate`
- `below` 删除（考卷上没有"below"）
- 动态用 `how...changed`，静态用 `how...differ`
- 列举三项用破折号或冒号引出

---

## What Percentage 高级句式

普通写法（6分）：
```
The percentage of sodium in dinner is 43%.
43% of sodium is consumed in dinner.
```

高级写法（7+）：
```
What percentage of sodium is consumed in dinner amounts to 43%.
```

**公式**：
```
What percentage + of + 名词 + 动词 + from/in + 类别 + 动词 + 数据
```

**更多例句**：
- `What percentage of saturated fat comes from dinner constitutes 37%.`
- `What percentage of added sugar is taken in from snacks makes up 42%.`
- `What percentage of sodium is consumed in breakfast and snacks are the same, with each accounting for 14%.`

---

## Overview 三条线策略

饼状图 Overview 核心方法：**三条线策略**

| 策略 | 说明 | 范例句式 |
|------|------|----------|
| ① 共同最大来源 | 找出所有饼图共同的最大值来源 | `...is the largest source of both sodium and saturated fat` |
| ② 特殊模式 | 指出反常/特殊的数据分布 | `By contrast, snacks contain the highest proportion of added sugar` |
| ③ 共同最小值 | 找出所有饼图共同的最小值 | `Breakfast accounts for the smallest percentage of all three nutrients` |

### 完整范文 Overview

> ==Overall,== it is clear that dinner is the largest source of both sodium and saturated fat, ==whereas== snacks contain the highest proportion of added sugar. Breakfast accounts for the smallest percentage of all three nutrients, while the figures for lunch are relatively moderate across the three categories.

---

## 完整范文（4段）

### P1 — Sodium + Saturated fat（大 + 等）

> Commencing with sodium intake, the largest percentage comes from dinner at 43%, as opposed to 29% from lunch. The figures for breakfast and snacks are the same, with each accounting for 14%. Similarly, what percentage of saturated fat is consumed in dinner makes up 37%, which is the largest among the four meal types, followed by lunch at 26%. Snacks contribute 21% of saturated fat intake, slightly higher than the 16% recorded for breakfast.

> **段落逻辑**：先写 sodium（大→小），再写 saturated fat（大→小）。两个饼图结构相似（dinner最大），用 `similarly` 连接。

### P2 — Added sugar（特殊模式：小 → 倍）

> By contrast, the pattern for added sugar is markedly different. What percentage of added sugar is taken in from snacks constitutes 42%, nearly twice as high as that from dinner at 23%. Lunch accounts for 19% of added sugar consumption, which is slightly higher than the 16% observed for breakfast. This makes snacks the dominant source of added sugar, standing in sharp contrast to the patterns seen with sodium and saturated fat.

> **段落逻辑**：强调 added sugar 的特殊性 — snacks 最大而非 dinner。用 `twice as high as` 表达倍数关系。

### P3 — 补充对比（段间过渡）

> With regard to the overall distribution, it is notable that breakfast consistently represents the smallest share across all three nutrients, ranging from 14% to 16%. Lunch, on the other hand, shows a relatively stable pattern, contributing between 19% and 29% of the three nutrients. Concerning dinner, while it is the primary source for sodium and saturated fat, it only accounts for 19% of added sugar intake, which is lower than both snacks and lunch.

> **段落逻辑**：横向总结 breakfast / lunch / dinner 在不同营养素中的表现，用 `With regard to` / `Concerning` 切换话题。

## 等批改对比（6分 → 7分）

### 低分写法（6分）

```
The figures for breakfast and snacks are both 14%.
→ The figure for breakfast equals that for snacks, both at 14%.
→ The percentage of sodium in breakfast is equal to that in snacks.
```

问题：`equal to` 语法正确但平淡；`both at 14%` 过于口语化。

### 高分写法（7分）

```
The figures for breakfast and snacks are the same, with each accounting for 14%.
```

升级要点：
- `equal/equals` → `are the same`
- 追加独立主格结构：`, with each accounting for...`
- `account for` 替代 `is/are`，更加学术化

### 更多升级对比

| 6分 | 7分 |
|-----|-----|
| The figure for A equals that for B. | The figures for A and B are the same, with each accounting for X%. |
| Both are 14%. | Each accounts for 14%. |
| A is equal to B. | A is identical to B in terms of percentage. |
| 43% of sodium is in dinner. | What percentage of sodium is consumed in dinner amounts to 43%. |

## 大小等倍表格

| 关系 | 处理方式 | 核心表达 |
|------|----------|----------|
| **大** | 最大值 | `the largest source / the highest proportion / accounts for the largest percentage` |
| **小** | 最小值 | `the smallest share / the lowest figure / represents the smallest proportion` |
| **等** | 相等 | `are the same, with each accounting for...` / `is identical to` |
| **倍** | 倍数 | `twice as high as` / `nearly double that of` / `three times as much as` |
| **比…高** | 稍高 | `slightly higher than` / `marginally more than` |
| **比…低** | 稍低 | `slightly lower than` / `marginally less than` |

### 本题目具体应用

| 营养素 | 大 | 小 | 等 | 倍 |
|--------|---|----|----|-----|
| Sodium | dinner 43% | breakfast/snacks 14% | breakfast = snacks | dinner ≈ 3× breakfast |
| Saturated fat | dinner 37% | breakfast 16% | — | dinner ≈ 2.3× breakfast |
| Added sugar | snacks 42% | breakfast 16% | — | snacks ≈ 2.2× dinner / snacks ≈ 2.6× breakfast |

## 关键表达汇总

### 静态图核心表达

| 类别 | 表达 | 例句 |
|------|------|------|
| 占比例 | `account for` | Dinner accounts for 43% of sodium intake. |
| 占比例 | `constitute` | Snacks constitute 42% of added sugar. |
| 占比例 | `make up` | Lunch makes up 29% of sodium consumption. |
| 占比例 | `contribute` | Breakfast contributes only 14% of sodium. |
| 占比例 | `represent` | Dinner represents the largest share. |

### 对比与转折

| 表达 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| `as opposed to` | 对比两个不同数据：`...dinner at 43%, as opposed to 29% from lunch` |
| `whereas` | 从句级对比：`Dinner is the largest source, whereas breakfast is the smallest` |
| `by contrast` | 段间/句间强烈对比：`By contrast, the pattern for added sugar is markedly different` |
| `on the other hand` | 另一方面 |
| `while` | 并列对比：`...43% for dinner, while only 14% for breakfast` |

### 程度修饰

| 表达 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| `slightly higher than` | 稍高 |
| `marginally more than` | 略高 |
| `considerably higher than` | 显著高 |
| `nearly twice as high as` | 近两倍 |
| `almost double that of` | 几乎是…的两倍 |
| `substantially lower than` | 大幅低于 |

### 段落/话题切换

| 表达 | 用法 | 位置 |
|------|------|------|
| `commencing with` | 开始讨论第一个饼图/类别 | 段首 |
| `with regard to` | 关于… / 就…而言 | 段首/句中 |
| `concerning` | 关于… / 涉及… | 段首/句中 |
| `in terms of` | 在…方面 | 句中 |
| `similarly` | 类似地（连接相似模式） | 段首/句中 |
| `by contrast` | 相反（连接不同模式） | 段首 |

## 百分比语法规则

### 规则一：百分比 + of + 名词

```
43% of sodium intake comes from dinner.      ✅
43% sodium intake comes from dinner.          ❌ (缺少 of)
```

### 规则二：百分比作主语时，动词由 of 后面的名词决定

```
43% of sodium is consumed in dinner.           ✅ (sodium 不可数 → 单数)
43% of the meals are consumed at home.         ✅ (meals 可数复数 → 复数)
14% of the intake comes from snacks.          ✅ (intake 单数)
```

### 规则三：百分比前面不要加 the（除非特指）

```
43% of sodium comes from dinner.              ✅
The 43% of sodium comes from dinner.           ❌ (不需要 the)

The percentage of sodium from dinner is 43%.  ✅ (percentage 前可以加 the)
The figure for dinner stands at 43%.           ✅ (figure 前可以加 the)
```

### 规则四：百分比的动词搭配

| 搭配 | 例句 |
|------|------|
| `account for + 百分比` | Dinner accounts for 43%. |
| `make up + 百分比` | Snacks make up 42%. |
| `constitute + 百分比` | Lunch constitutes 29%. |
| `stand at + 百分比` | The figure stands at 14%. |
| `amount to + 百分比` | What percentage from dinner amounts to 43%. |
| `be + 百分比` | The percentage is 14%. |

### 规则五：多个百分比并列

```
The figures for breakfast and snacks are the same, with each accounting for 14%.

→ 独立主格结构：, with + 名词 + 现在分词
→ 比 "and both are 14%" 更高级
```

### 规则六：百分比范围的表达

```
Breakfast accounts for between 14% and 16% of all three nutrients.   ✅
Breakfast accounts for 14% to 16% of all three nutrients.            ✅
Lunch contributes from 19% to 29%.                                   ✅
```

## 静态图写作流程总结

```
1. Introduction   → Given are charts showing how...differ in...
2. Overview       → 三条线：共同最大来源 + 特殊模式 + 共同最小值
3. P1（常规模式） → 按大小等倍顺序描述结构相似的饼图
4. P2（特殊模式） → 描述反常的饼图，用 by contrast
5. P3（补充对比） → 横向总结，用 with regard to / concerning
```

> 全程一般现在时。无时间变化，关注的是「不同类别之间的差异」。

## 易错提醒

| 易错点 | 错误 | 正确 |
|--------|------|------|
| 时态 | used past tense | **全部一般现在时** |
| 百分比 of | 43% sodium | **43% of sodium** |
| 倍数 | two times higher | **twice as high as** |
| 相等 | equal to | **are the same, with each accounting for** |
| 段首 | Firstly | **Commencing with / With regard to** |
| 动态词 | increased / declined | **accounts for / constitutes** |
